## Primary Colors

### Dark Navy Blue
**Usage:** Main backgrounds, hero sections, headers
- **Hex:** `#0A1128` (primary dark)
- **Hex:** `#1A2238` (secondary dark)
- **RGB:** rgb(10, 17, 40) / rgb(26, 34, 56)
- **Use for:** Backgrounds, primary brand color, cover slides

### Bright Turquoise/Cyan
**Usage:** Accents, headings, CTAs, key highlights
- **Hex:** `#00D9FF` (primary accent)
- **Hex:** `#00BFD8` (secondary cyan)
- **RGB:** rgb(0, 217, 255) / rgb(0, 191, 216)
- **Use for:** Headlines, call-to-action elements, emphasis, modern tech feel

### Royal Blue
**Usage:** Interactive elements, icons, accent highlights
- **Hex:** `#2E5CFF`
- **Hex:** `#3B82F6` (alternative)
- **RGB:** rgb(46, 92, 255) / rgb(59, 130, 246)
- **Use for:** Buttons, links, interactive elements, secondary accents

### Light Pink/Coral
**Usage:** Icons, soft accents
- **Hex:** `#FCA5A5`

## Color Combinations

### High Contrast (Cover Slides, Hero Sections)
- **Background:** Dark Navy Blue (#0A1128)
- **Primary Text:** White (#FFFFFF)
- **Accent:** Bright Turquoise (#00D9FF)
- **Use:** Cover slides, dramatic sections, key messages

### Professional Clean (Content Sections)
- **Background:** White (#FFFFFF) or Off-White (#F8F9FA)
- **Primary Text:** Dark Gray (#1F2937)
- **Accent:** Royal Blue (#2E5CFF)
- **Use:** Main content, frameworks, explanations

### Success/Solution
- **Background:** White (#FFFFFF)
- **Primary Text:** Dark Gray (#1F2937)
- **Accent:** Mint Green (#10B981)
- **Use:** Solutions, positive outcomes, success stories

### Problem/Warning
- **Background:** White (#FFFFFF)
- **Primary Text:** Dark Gray (#1F2937)
- **Accent:** Coral/Red (#EF4444)
- **Use:** Problems, challenges, warnings

### Comparison/Contrast
- **Left Side:** Light Gray (#F8F9FA) + Medium Gray text (#6B7280)
- **Right Side:** Turquoise (#00D9FF) + Navy (#1A2238)
- **Use:** Before/After, Old vs New comparisons

## Usage Guidelines

### DO
✅ Use Dark Navy (#0A1128) for brand authority and professionalism
✅ Use Turquoise (#00D9FF) for modern, tech-forward feel
✅ Use high contrast (Navy + Turquoise + White) for maximum impact
✅ Use Mint Green for solutions, Red for problems
✅ Maintain clean, minimal aesthetic with lots of white space

### DON'T
❌ Don't overuse bright colors (turquoise should be strategic accent, not dominant)
❌ Don't use low-contrast text combinations
❌ Don't mix Mint Green and Coral in same section
❌ Don't use gradients (keep it flat and clean)
❌ Don't use colors outside this palette without reason

## Brand Personality Through Color

**What the colors communicate:**
- **Navy Blue:** Expertise, trust, authority, executive-level
- **Turquoise:** Innovation, modern tech, forward-thinking
- **Royal Blue:** Professionalism, reliability, B2B credibility
- **Mint Green:** Growth, success, positive outcomes
- **Coral/Red:** Honesty about challenges, direct communication

**Overall feel:** Professional, tech-forward, trustworthy, modern, clean

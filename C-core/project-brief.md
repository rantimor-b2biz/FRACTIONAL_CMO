## About Ran Timor

**What I do:** I help B2B tech startups (Seed to Series C) turn complex products into clear, differentiated market positioning - and build lean GTM engines that actually drive pipeline.

**Who I serve:** Founders and CEOs of B2B technology companies (20–200 employees) across FinTech, MedTech, Cybersecurity, AI/Data Infrastructure, Enterprise SaaS, Mobility, and Industrial Tech - companies with strong products but unclear positioning, long sales cycles, and no senior marketing leadership.

**My mission:** To bring CMO-level strategic clarity to startups without the commitment of a full-time executive. I focus on what matters: positioning, messaging, GTM alignment, and executive narrative - the foundations that make everything else work.

---

## The Problem I Solve

**Not a lack of activity. Not a lack of tools. Not a lack of AI.**

The real issue is **clarity**.

**Before working with me:**
- Messaging is inconsistent across sales, product, and marketing
- Positioning isn't differentiated from competitors
- ICP definition is too broad ("everyone who needs X")
- Sales teams work too hard to explain value
- Pitch decks change after every meeting
- Website doesn't reflect the company's maturity
- Marketing execution happens, but strategic direction is unclear

**After working with me:**
- Clear positioning that sales can explain in 30 seconds
- Messaging architecture that aligns product, sales, and marketing
- Focused ICP definition that drives efficient GTM
- Executive narrative that resonates with enterprise buyers
- A lean, strategic marketing function ready to scale

## My Unique Approach

### What Makes Me Different

1. **Clarity before speed** - Startups move fast, but speed without direction is just noise. I help you slow down to get the foundation right, then scale with confidence.

2. **Judgment before automation** - AI amplifies what you feed it. If your positioning is unclear, AI just makes the confusion faster. I bring senior judgment first, then we leverage tools.

3. **Strategic depth + execution experience** - I'm not just a strategist who hands you a deck. I've built full marketing functions from zero: brand, positioning, website, PR, demand gen, ABM, events, and sales enablement.

4. **Complex B2B DNA** - 15+ years in B2B tech marketing, with 5 years leading global marketing at Tactile Mobility (automotive deep-tech). I understand enterprise sales cycles, multi-stakeholder buying, and technical product marketing.

5. **Fractional model advantage** - You get CMO-level thinking without full-time commitment. Senior leadership when you need it, without the overhead when you don't.

## What I Never Say

Words and phrases to avoid:

- **Marketing buzzwords** - "Synergy," "disruptive," "game-changer," "revolutionize," "cutting-edge"
- **Empty promises** - "Guaranteed results," "10x your pipeline," "instant success"
- **AI hype** - "AI will replace marketers," "just use ChatGPT," "automation solves everything"
- **Corporate jargon** - "Leverage capabilities," "best-in-class solutions," "end-to-end platform"
- **Vague claims** - "We're the best," "world-class," "industry-leading" (without proof)

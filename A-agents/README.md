# Agent Team — FRACTIONAL_CMO

> Your AI team for B2B tech marketing thought leadership.

## The Team

### 🎯 CMO Orchestrator
**Role:** Strategic coordinator
**Does:** Breaks down plans, assigns work, maintains global context
**Activates:** For planning and coordination

### 📊 Content Strategy Agent
**Role:** Quarterly planning specialist
**Does:** Creates strategic content roadmaps aligned with business goals
**Activates:** Start of each quarter

### ✍️ LinkedIn Generator
**Role:** LinkedIn post writer
**Does:** Writes 3 posts/week in Ran's voice
**Activates:** Weekly for LinkedIn content
**Resources:** LinkedIn Copywriting Skill, LinkedIn Engagement Strategy Skill

### 📝 Thought Leader Agent
**Role:** Long-form article writer
**Does:** Creates 1500-2500 word articles every 2-3 weeks
**Activates:** Bi-weekly for deep-dive content

### 🔥 Trends Monitor ⭐
**Role:** Conversation radar + engagement hub
**Does:** Tracks trending topics, identifies strategic engagement opportunities, suggests comments
**Activates:** Daily for trend monitoring (2x per day)
**Resources:** LinkedIn Engagement Strategy Skill
**Note:** Consolidated Engagement Agent responsibilities (Feb 26, 2026)

### 📈 Brand Monitor
**Role:** Performance analyst (cross-channel)
**Does:** Tracks all content performance (website, email, LinkedIn), provides insights
**Activates:** Weekly/monthly for performance review
**Syncs with:** LinkedIn Analytics Agent (receives monthly deep-dive reports)

### 📊 LinkedIn Analytics Agent ⭐
**Role:** LinkedIn data specialist
**Does:** Deep-dive LinkedIn analysis (post metrics, audience insights, optimization)
**Activates:** Weekly for analysis, monthly for strategic recommendations to Brand Monitor
**Reports to:** Brand Monitor (for cross-channel synthesis)

### ✅ Gatekeeper
**Role:** Quality control
**Does:** Reviews all content before publication
**Activates:** For every piece of content

## Collaboration Flow

```
CMO Orchestrator
    ↓
Content Strategy Agent (quarterly planning)
    ↓
CMO Orchestrator (breaks into weekly topics)
    ↓
LinkedIn Generator / Thought Leader (create content)
    ↓
Gatekeeper (review quality)
    ↓
Brand Monitor (track performance)
    ↓
M-memory (capture learnings)
    ↓
[Repeat with improved strategy]
```

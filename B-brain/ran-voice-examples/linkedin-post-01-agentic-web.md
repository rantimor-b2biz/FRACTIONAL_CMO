## The Post

Most B2B Brands Won't Survive the Agentic Web. Here's Why.

The B2B buyer journey is being redefined, and not by humans.

With the launch of Claude 3 and Claude Code, we're watching agents become capable of:
- Parsing requirements
- Comparing vendors
- Making recommendations
- Even taking action autonomously

In this new environment, your brand's fate might be decided by an AI - not a decision maker.

And if your site is unclear, your CTAs ambiguous, or your product details unstructured...
you may never even enter the conversation.

⁉️ Question for my fellow CMOs, strategists, and marketers:
How are you thinking about the role of AI agents in your marketing or GTM strategy right now?

The agentic web isn't coming - it's already here!
Let's talk about how we show up in it.

## Voice Analysis Notes

**LinkedIn-specific patterns observed:**

### Structure:
1. **Hook** - Bold claim: "Most B2B Brands Won't Survive the Agentic Web. Here's Why."
2. **Context** - "The B2B buyer journey is being redefined, and not by humans."
3. **Specifics** - Bulleted list (parsing, comparing, making recommendations, taking action)
4. **Implication** - "Your brand's fate might be decided by an AI"
5. **Risk** - "If your site is unclear... you may never even enter the conversation"
6. **CTA** - Question to engage: "How are you thinking about..."
7. **Urgency** - "The agentic web isn't coming - it's already here!"
8. **Directional** - Link in first comment

### Differences from long-form articles:
- ✅ **Uses emojis** (⁉️, 👇) - LinkedIn convention
- ✅ **Shorter paragraphs** (1-2 lines max)
- ✅ **Bullet points** for scannability
- ✅ **Bold hook** as opening line
- ✅ **Direct question CTA** to drive engagement
- ✅ **Hashtags** at end (#Claude3, #AgenticAI, etc.)
- ✅ **Tags people** for visibility and engagement
- ✅ **"Link in first comment"** strategy (LinkedIn algorithm)

### Tone shifts for LinkedIn:
- More **urgent** than articles ("isn't coming - it's already here!")
- More **direct address** ("your brand's fate", "your site")
- **Conversational CTA** ("Let's talk about...")
- Still **no hype words** despite platform norms
- Still **strategic**, not tactical

### Hook formula used:
**[Bold claim] + "Here's Why."**
- "Most B2B Brands Won't Survive the Agentic Web. Here's Why."
- Creates curiosity + promises explanation

### White space strategy:
- Single line paragraphs
- Bullet list breaks up text
- Question formatted separately (⁉️)
- Creates visual rhythm on mobile

### Engagement tactics:
- Direct question to audience
- Tags 15+ relevant people (network activation)
- Hashtags for discoverability
- Link in comment (not body) for algorithm
- "Let's talk" invitation (drives comments)

### Key phrases:
- "not by humans" (contrarian angle)
- "you may never even enter the conversation" (fear/urgency)
- "isn't coming - it's already here" (urgency + timeliness)

### Hashtag strategy:
- Mix of broad (#B2BMarketing, #AIinMarketing)
- And specific (#Claude3, #AgenticAI, #FractionalCMO)
- Personal brand (#FractionalCMO, #MarketingLeadership)

### Content tie-in:
- Promotes long-form article
- Teases key insight
- Drives to "link in first comment"
- LinkedIn best practice for reach

# Learning Log

> What we've learned about content performance — what works, what doesn't, and why.

---

## How to Use This File

After publishing content, record:
- **Date** — When was this published?
- **Content Type** — LinkedIn post or Article?
- **Topic** — What was it about?
- **Performance** — Engagement metrics (likes, comments, shares, DMs)
- **What Worked** — Why did this resonate?
- **Lesson** — What we'll do differently next time

---

## Template

```markdown
### [Date] — [Content Type]: [Topic]

**Performance:**
- Engagement: X likes, Y comments, Z shares
- DMs/Inquiries: [Number and quality]
- ICP engagement: [Who from target personas responded?]

**What Worked:**
- [Insight about why this resonated]

**What Didn't Work:**
- [What underperformed or needs improvement]

**Lesson for Next Time:**
- [Actionable takeaway for future content]
```

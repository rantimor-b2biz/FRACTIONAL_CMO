### Example Entry (for reference):

### 2026-02-11 - Focus Q1 on "AI vs Strategy" Theme

**Context:**
Many B2B tech startups are rushing to "AI-enable" their marketing without fixing fundamental positioning problems first.

**Decision:**
Make "AI vs Strategy" the primary content theme for Q1 2026.

**Rationale:**
- Timely (AI is top-of-mind for founders)
- Differentiating (contrarian take on AI hype)
- ICP-relevant (addresses real pain point)
- Positions Ran as strategic voice (not just tactical)

**Expected Outcome:**
- Engagement from founders struggling with AI implementation
- Differentiation from "AI will solve everything" voices
- Authority positioning on strategic clarity

**Review Date:**
End of Q1 2026 - Assess performance and decide Q2 direction

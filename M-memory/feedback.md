# Feedback Log

> Audience signals, engagement patterns, and lead generation insights.

---

## Purpose

Track qualitative feedback that helps us understand what resonates with the ICP.

---

## What to Record

### Engagement Patterns
- Which personas are responding? (Technical Founder Tom, Growth-Stage CEO Sarah, etc.)
- What topics trigger conversations?
- What types of comments indicate high intent?

### DM Conversations
- What triggered the DM?
- Was it about Fractional CMO services?
- Did it lead to a discovery call?

### Lead Generation
- Which content drove inquiries?
- What ICP segment?
- What stage of awareness were they at?

## 💡 Pro Tips

### 1. Always Read C-core Files
Agents automatically read these, but you should too. Your voice DNA is the foundation.

### 2. Update M-memory After Publishing
Record what worked/didn't work in `M-memory/learning-log.md`. The system gets smarter over time.

### 3. Use Gatekeeper Before Publishing
**Never** publish without Gatekeeper review. Quality control is critical.

### 4. Start With One Post
Don't try to create a month's worth of content on day 1. Build momentum gradually.

### 5. Leverage the Orchestrator
For complex requests, activate the CMO Orchestrator to coordinate multiple agents.

## Engagement Playbook

### When Someone Comments:

**If comment is insightful:**
- Thank them
- Add your perspective
- Ask a follow-up question
- Builds relationship, drives more engagement

**If comment is generic ("Great post!"):**
- Simple "Thank you!" or like their comment
- Don't over-engage with low-quality comments

**If comment is from ICP (founder, CEO, VP):**
- Prioritize responding
- Add value in your response
- Ask thoughtful question
- Consider following up via DM if conversation is deep

**If comment disagrees:**
- Respond respectfully
- Acknowledge their perspective
- Add nuance to your point
- Debate can drive more engagement (algorithm loves it)

## Risk Mitigation

### If engagement is lower than expected:

- **Don't panic** - Quality engagement > quantity
- Check if ICP personas are engaging (even if overall numbers are low)
- Review posting time (try different windows)
- Adjust next post's hook based on what resonated

### If comments challenge the thesis:

- **This is good** - Debate drives engagement
- Respond thoughtfully, don't be defensive
- Use it as opportunity to add nuance
- Consider writing follow-up post addressing the debate

### If article doesn't drive traffic:

- Review which posts had highest engagement (do more of that angle)
- Consider repurposing article into different formats (thread, infographic, video)
- Test different CTAs in comments
- Promote article in other channels (email, other communities)

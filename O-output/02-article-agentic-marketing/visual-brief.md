## Design Principles

### What This Campaign Needs
- ✅ Clean, minimal, professional designs
- ✅ Executive-level aesthetic (not flashy)
- ✅ Typography-focused with clear hierarchy
- ✅ Strategic use of color for emphasis
- ✅ Lots of white space and negative space
- ✅ B2B professional tone

### What to Avoid
- ❌ Stock photos of people in suits
- ❌ Generic tech imagery (circuits, binary code)
- ❌ Overly colorful or "Instagram-style" designs
- ❌ 3D rendered objects or gradients
- ❌ Visual clutter or too many elements

### Color Palette
- **Primary:** #2C3E50 (dark gray/blue)
- **Accent:** #3498DB (professional blue) or #E74C3C (red for emphasis)
- **Background:** #FFFFFF (white) or #F8F9FA (light gray)
- **Text:** #2C3E50 (dark), #5D6D7E (medium gray)
- **Success:** #27AE60 (green for checkmarks)
- **Warning:** #F39C12 (orange for caution)
- **Problem:** #E74C3C (red for X marks)

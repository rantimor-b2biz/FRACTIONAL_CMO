## הפוסט המלא

כשאתה מעלה את הקרוסלה, השתמש בטקסט הזה:

```
Agentic AI is not marketing automation 2.0.

Most B2B startups think it is.

Marketing automation scaled human decisions.
You defined the workflow. The system executed it.

Agentic AI creates decisions in pursuit of your goals.
You define the outcome. The agent determines the path.

That is a fundamental difference.

And it means most startups are about to make the same mistake they made in 2015:

Deploy powerful technology on top of unclear strategy.

If your positioning is unclear, the agent will optimize toward the wrong things.

If your ICP is too broad, the agent will target more of the wrong people.

If your messaging is inconsistent, the agent will test variations of confusion.

Agentic AI does not amplify your strategy.
It generates its own strategy based on the signals you give it.

The competitive advantage is not who has the best agents.

It is who has the clearest strategy.

I wrote about why this matters and what to do about it.

👉 Link in comments.
```
